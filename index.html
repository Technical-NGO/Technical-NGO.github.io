Who are we?

We are a diverse cast of people, from various walks of life and from all over the world. What unites us is our love for web development.

For free?! Really?

Yup! But only if you're a not-for-profit organization/group.
But why free?
Well, for one, it's our way of saying thank you for all the amazing work you're doing.
Two, we all got into web development as we love it. So this is just a way for us to continue doing what we love, while at the same time, giving back to the society.

What we do

Somewhere far away, there is a huge airconditioned place with floor to ceiling shelves/racks. And on each of these racks, there are lots of disks, each measured in terabytes. Here, with a bit of magic, we get you a few megabytes of your own, that you can call home.
Basically, we design websites. That's what we do.
